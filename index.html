If you are out of campus, first you need to download and connet to the Ivanti VPN before connect to the Brandeis server. Here is a link to the VPN tutorial:

Brandeis Ivanti VPN Tutorial

  • Click “Go” button at the left top of your screen

  • Click “Connect to Server” in the Go Menu.

  • Enter “smb://files.brandeis.edu” as the name of server and click “connect”.

  • Enter Your Brandeis Username and Password.

  • Find “dsi” from the volumes and click “OK

  • The dsi folder will show on your screen, click “student_leave_of_absence folder

  • Want more information about our project?
